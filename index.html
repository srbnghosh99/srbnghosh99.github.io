--- layout: minimal title: My Website --- I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, working with Dr. Erik Saule. My research revolves around machine learning, text mining, and large graph mining. --- #### Research Interests **My research agenda is based on three pillars:** - **Graph Representation Learning** Developing models to understand and extract meaningful structures from complex networks, including analyzing downstream tasks such as community detection, link prediction, and node classification and visualization. - **Applied Machine Learning for Social Impact** Leveraging NLP and network science to analyze real-world problems such as transportation safety, hate speech detection, and misinformation using social media and scholarly data. - **Bias, Fairness, and Trust in Scholarly and Social Networks** Investigating disparities in collaboration patterns, author networks, and online discourse using graph- and NLP-based techniques to uncover hidden bias and promote equitable systems.
